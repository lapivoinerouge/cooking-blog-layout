Ingredients (4 medium Pizzas):

Dough:

  • 4 cups (0.5 kg) Tipo 00 flour
  • 1 teaspoon (5 g) salt
  • 1 1/4 cups (325 ml) warm water
  • 2 teaspoons (8 g) instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon (10 g) of olive oil
  • semolina to sprinkle the dough

Topping:

  • Perfect Pizza Sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozarella cheese

Instructions:

Turn on the oven and preheat a pizza stone (250-270Â°C). Combine dry ingredients in a bowl. Add water and olive oil. Water has to be warm! Otherwise the dough is ruined! Stir until the dough is formed. Divide into 4 pieces. Stretch the dough into circle and sprinkle with semolina on the bottom and on the sides. Spread the sauce and add shredded mozarella on top.

Now is time for you to be creative. You can use some Prosciutto, shredded Parmigiano Reggiano and Rucola. If you like to experiment you can pour some fig sauce or put slices of pear and wallnuts on top. If you would rather use some chicken and corn - go ahead. But please, no matter how bad your day was, do not eat it with ketchup.
